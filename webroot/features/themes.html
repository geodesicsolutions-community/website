Completely Customizable Themes
Use "as is" or as a Starting Point for your own design!

It's really that easy! All of the themes below come automatically installed with GeoCoreCE. Simply click on the theme you want to use. Want to move things around in the theme? No problem. GeoCoreCE's themes are built using basic HTML, which you have access to, like an ordinary website.

Mobile Friendly - All of our themes make use of RWD. What is RWD? RWD stands for "Responsive Web Design" which means that the design has been formatted in a way that is most optimal for viewing and navigation across a wide range of devices, including PCs, smartphones and tablets... in other words, GeoCoreCE themes automatically reformat their designs to fit the device that your visitor is using to view your site... Mobile Friendly!
