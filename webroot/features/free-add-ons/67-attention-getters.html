The Attention Getters Add On offers you the ability to generate additional income through your site by charging users a premium to have "additional exposure" added to their listings through attention getters. When a seller lists their item, one of the options will be to select an "attention getter". The fee to add the attention getter to their listing will be displayed to them at that time. Charge any fee you like.

Once installed the Add On has its own image upload form that allows you to add (and delete) as many attention getters as you want to offer as a choice for your users. You can completely replace the default Attention Getters that come with the Add On and add your own. As soon as an attention getter is added through the form, it will automatically appear as a choice to your sellers...nothing more for you to do!
