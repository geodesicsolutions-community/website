With the Signs & Flyers Add On your customers will have the ability to easily print a sign and/or flyer through a dynamically generated form for each active listing they have placed on your site. Customize the sign and flyer templates to your liking or use the default templates we provide. Each sign and flyer they print can display your domain name on it...FREE ADVERTISING FOR YOU whenever they post one of these printouts somewhere!

The default templates can be easily modified to display the design and content that you want. The classified ad, directory, or auction data is displayed through the use of simple 'tags' that allow you to place each field exactly where you want them to appear in the template.

When the seller selects to print a sign or flyer from their "My Account" section, the listing's content will automatically populate in the form's fields. Makes it super easy for your users to generate a sign or a flyer using the listing data!

In some cases, the seller may not have uploaded a photo of the item along with their listing. Therefore, you can upload a default photo (or set of photos if you like) for them to select from which will print out in their Sign or Flyer.
